Scenario Companion

らっしゃい！トレセン軒！ ~The Retribution of Kindness~

🍜

The F2P Success Formula

This scenario focuses heavily on coordinating your Tasting Parties with your major training turns. Because F2P players often struggle with low support card raw training values, using the correct regional buffs at high-stack moments is critical to breaking your training caps.

💡

Top Rental Priority

Always borrow SSR Tazuna Hayakawa [一杯のノスタルジア]. She raises your default Learning Gauge gain per turn from 3 to 10, hands out two free Secret Ingredients during date interactions, and maxes out your gauge every turn during Summer Camp.

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

Phase 1: Junior Era Turns 1 - 11

Setup, Gauges & Early Region Choice

🎯 Region Selection (Turn 3): Choose Sapporo, Tokyo, and Hakodate.

Reason: Sapporo gives Speed Training bonuses, Tokyo boosts Wisdom training, and Hakodate provides Stamina support. This aligns perfectly with common F2P Speed/Wisdom training decks.

📈 Gameplan: Build your support card relationships early. Avoid using Tricks for Tasting Parties immediately; save them until you have 3 or more support cards grouped on a single highly-boosted stat track.
Phase 2: Classic Era Turns 12 - 35

Friendship Training & Summer Camp Setup

🎯 Region Selection (After 1st RMJ): Choose Kyoto, Hanshin, and Nakayama.

Reason: Kyoto and Hanshin unlock dual-stat Friendship bonuses in Classic. These stack with your support cards once they trigger rainbow training, allowing F2P decks to hit massive stat gains.

🏖️ Summer Camp Prep (June, Turn 1): Stop triggering Tasting Parties in May. Bank up to 4 or 5 Tricks. When Summer Camp begins, the Tazuna card automatically maxes out your Learning Gauge every turn. Cook every turn of camp to keep the camp multiplier active constantly.
Phase 3: Senior Era Turns 36 - 47

Maxing Caps & Excitement Accumulation

🎯 Region Selection (After 2nd RMJ): Re-select Sapporo, Tokyo, and a third region of your choice.

Reason: During Senior year, single-stat regions receive massive enhancements. Sapporo now boosts Friendship Speed bonuses and activates an extra copy of Speed Support Cards in training, helping you reach the 2150 Speed cap.

🔥 Jamboree Goal: Ensure you earn enough Excitement Points before December to comfortably surpass the 3500 or 5000 point milestones. This secures the prestigious Super RMJ EX evaluation and applies massive permanent training buffs.
Final Phase: URA Finals URA Weeks

Choosing Your Ultimate Ramen

🍜 Choose based on your stat requirements:
  • Top (Special) Ramen: Best for Stamina/Guts builds. Grants massive cap limit breaks (+100) and the "火事場のバ鹿力" rare skill hint.
  • Middle (Yokubari) Ramen: Best for standard Speed/Power builds. Grants Speed, Stamina, Power, and Wisdom limit breaks (+100) and the "いいとこ入った！" rare skill hint.
  • Bottom (Shugoku) Ramen: Best for Speed/Power/Guts/Wisdom builds. Grants Speed, Power, Guts, and Wisdom limit breaks (+100) and the "好奇心" rare skill hint.
