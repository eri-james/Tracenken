The Story

WHY RAMEN?

Wanting to repay their fans with ramen, Umamusumes decided to hold a nationwide ramen event. With support from Tazuna Hayakawa and Light Hello, Tracen-ken now opens its doors!

Training Goal

YOUR OBJECTIVE

Your goal is participating in the Twinkle Series, as well as training your ramen skills to lead the nationwide ramen event to success.

At the end of the year, the Ramen Jamboree is held to check your progress.

Region Selection

10 Regions, 10 Bowls

Choose 3 regions each year. Each offers a unique ramen recipe with buffs for your training.

First Region Selection at Turn #3. Reselect after each Ramen Jamboree (excluding 3rd RMJ). Junior: 5 regions. Classic: 5 regions. Senior: all 10 regions.

How It Works

Learning Gauges increase every turn (except training failed / skipped by lazy habit) by the amount of base Learning Gauge gain.

Each training facility has a random bubble of an ingredient assigned every turn, boosting that ingredient's gain. Regions also boost one ingredient's gauge gain.

Non-support card characters, Friend / Group Support Cards in training, and Friendship Training all boost Learning Gauge gain.

EXAMPLE GAUGE (7 = 1 Trick)
5 / 7 — train more to earn a Trick!
  • 7 gauge = 1 Trick of that ingredient type
  • Gauge resets after hitting 7 (no overlap)
  • Hold max 10 Tricks at once
  • Overflow discards the oldest Trick
  • Tricks discarded after each RMJ

Tasting Party

Activate via the button on the left side of the training screen. Use Tricks to cook ramen and receive Region Effects of the chosen recipe.

  • Each recipe requires different Tricks
  • Secret Ingredients substitute any Trick
  • Up to 2 Secret Ingredients per Tasting Party
  • Hold max 4 Secret Ingredients at once
  • NOT discarded after RMJ (unlike Tricks)

Trick Disposal

Ramen Jamboree discards all currently owned Tricks. They are used up to increase Energy recovery from the event (formula unknown).
